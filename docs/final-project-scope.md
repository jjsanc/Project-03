# Project Scope Statement
## Project Title: Personal Collectibles 

## Project Purpose
The purpose of this project is to build and maintain a personal collection of collectible item including enamel pins, cologne fragrances and Pokemon cards.The project aims to curate items that are valuable, rare, and personally meaningful.

## Project Objectives
- Build a collection of enamel pins (pop culture, travel, limited editions)
- A fragrance collection featuring designer and niche colognes.
- Expand a graded and/or mint-condition Pokemon cards.

## Desired Results
An organized collection of enamel pins, cologne and Pokemon cards.

## Collectible Categories
- Enamel pins (limited edition, pop culture, travel)
- Designer and niche colognes (samples, full bottles)
- Pokemon trading cards (Raw cards, graded cards, sealed products)

## Exclusions
- Collecting items outside of defined categories (pins, cologne, Pokemon cards). This includes action figures, posters or other memorabilia.
- Counterfeit or low-quality reproductions of collectible items.Only authentic, high-quality products are permitted.
- Items purchased purely for resale or investment purposes; all acquistions are for personal collection and enjoyment.
  
## Constraints
- Budget limitations
- Availability of limited items
- Market price fluctuations
- Storage space and proper preservation requirements for delicate items (pins, cologne, cards)
- Time availability: project progress depends on the collecters available time for acquisition and maintenance.
  
## Resourse Requirements
- Budget for purchasing items.
- Storage (shelves, binders, display cases)
- Acquisition resources: access to trusted online marketplace, retail stores and conventions.
  
## In Scope
Acquisition of Collectibles
- purchasing enamel pins (pop culture, limited editions)
- Buying designer and niche colognes
- obtaining Pokemon trading cards

Authentication and Quality control
- ensuring all collectibles are authentic and high-quality
- avoiding counterfeit or low-grade reproductions

## Out of Scope
Collectibles Outside Defined Categories
- Action figures, posters, comics, toys

Investment or Resale Activities
- purchasing items soley for financial gain or resale purposes
  

## Success Criteria
- Collection remains organized and well-preserved
- Items maintain good to excellent condition
- Achievement of a collection that reflects the owners personal interests and collecting goals.
